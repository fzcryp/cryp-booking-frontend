1. Introduction

Pbookings.com (“Pbookings”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates a travel search and comparison platform that allows users to explore, compare, and book travel-related services offered by third-party providers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data when you use our websites, mobile or web applications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”). It also explains your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable national data protection laws.

This Policy applies to all visitors and users of our Services.

2. Who We Are

Data Controller

The Pbookings.com entity responsible for the relevant Service acts as the data controller under GDPR.

Data Protection Contact / DPO

For questions relating to data protection or to exercise your rights, you may contact us at: privacy pbookings.com

You also have the right to contact your local data protection supervisory authority.

3. Our Business Model

Pbookings.com operates as a travel search, comparison, and facilitation platform. We connect users with third-party travel providers such as airlines, hotels, accommodation providers, car rental companies, package travel operators, and travel insurance providers (“Travel Partners”).

When you make or request a booking, relevant personal data must be shared with the applicable Travel Partner so they can deliver the requested service. Travel Partners act as independent data controllers once they receive your data.

4. Personal Data We Collect

4.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect:

  • Name, date of birth, age, gender
  • Email address, phone number, postal address
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Booking and travel details (itineraries, booking references, ports, dates, baggage, seat and meal preferences)
  • Passport details, citizenship, visa or redress numbers
  • Frequent flyer or loyalty numbers
  • Payment and billing information
  • Reviews, ratings, feedback, photos, and user-generated content
  • Communications with customer support
  • Information about other travelers you choose to include

Providing certain information is necessary to complete bookings or use specific features.

4.2 Sensitive Personal Data

Some data (e.g., passport details, health-related travel needs, dietary restrictions, or precise location data) may be considered sensitive under GDPR. Such data is processed only:

  • When necessary to provide Services, or
  • With your explicit consent

You may withdraw consent at any time.

4.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Device and browser data
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data (searches, clicks, interactions)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Precise location data is collected only with your permission.

4.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from:

  • Travel Partners
  • Group companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Advertising and analytics providers
  • Other users who share itineraries with you

This information may be combined with data we already hold.

4.5 Email Synchronization (Trips Feature)

If you enable email synchronization, we scan connected inboxes to identify travel confirmations. Extracted booking details and copies of relevant emails are stored in your Trips account. You may disable this feature at any time.
